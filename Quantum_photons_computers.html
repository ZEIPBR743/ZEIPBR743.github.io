Photonic Quantum Computer

Physicists and Mathematicians invented today's modern computer and its architecture. It was applying a specific physics property to create a machine that solves simple mathematical problems. But what if an alien society were to invent math and their own "autonomous machine", what kind of math theorem and algorithms would they choose to use? More interestely, what kind of physical property what they use to solve this problem?

Photon based quantum computer presented this question to the humanity. What if we invented computers using photons, instead of electons. What interesting algorithms would we use. Currently the photon quantum computer can only solve a very small amount of calculations in astonishing speed. What will photonic based technologies take us in the future?

Theoretical physicists and computer scientists will take us way back just like when Bardeen et al. invented vacuumed based transistors and perhaps will revelotionalize computing and communication through another physical medium or physical property. However, this change might take generations to accomplish. I cannot begin to fathom what would the new "Facebook" look like when I am in my 80s.
